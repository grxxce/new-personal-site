Don't give yourself exposure therapy to life.

There are a finite number of fun things you can do. The scary thing is, like drugs, they start to feel less fun over time thanks to habituation. Don't gorge out now. Life should be enjoyed bitter-sweet. Otherwise, you'll give yourself a toothache. Savour your time here.
