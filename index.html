Hey!

I’m Grace, and I love building the bridge between mind and machine. How can we make the computer feel like an extension of the brain? Seamless BCIs take extraordinary engineering and ingenious design—and I love working on all of it.

Here's what I'm up to now:

  • ML Researcher at Harvard Vision Computing Group working on a super-resolution representation of connectomics.
  • Building Accio, voice-first computer control for quadriplegics.
  • Exploring startups as a Roberts Family Fellow and Pear Venture Fellow.
  • Completing a Master’s degree in Computer Science and Bachelor's degree in Neuroscience at Harvard.

In the past, I have:

  • Worked on Photos, Memory Creation, and Apple Intelligence at Apple, selected to demo live to Craig Federighi (CTO of Apple).
  • Been a student-athlete on Harvard Varsity Sailing.
  • Research with Harvard Medical School investigating neurocognitive functions and MRI-based brain structural factors.
  • Co-Directed Harvard Tech for Social Good.

Some more fun things I've built:

  • A Beli for books / better Goodreads
  • Explainer video about Neural Stem Cells
